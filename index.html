Our Story

Founded on the principles of Independent Creation and Scent Integrity, Hyscent (HCYScent) was born to challenge the boundaries of mainstream perfumery. We specialize in four distinct olfactory journeys: the raw power of the Wild, the restrained luxury of Noble Leather, the golden opulence of the Arab desert, and the tranquil purity of Orient Zen.

As an original niche fragrance brand, we refuse to compromise on quality. Every bottle is a testament to our commitment to high-concentration oils, extreme longevity, and authentic aromatic experiences. Based in our China Sourcing Center, we serve a global community of connoisseurs across Europe, the Middle East, and the Americas.
