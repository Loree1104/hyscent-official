Rare Spices: The Heart of Arab Glow

The desert has many secrets, but none are as enchanting as its scents. Our "Arab Glow" collection was born from a desire to pay homage to the rich olfactory heritage of the Middle East, where perfume is not an accessory, but a profound cultural statement.

Oud: Liquid Gold

Known as "Liquid Gold," Oud is the resinous heart of the agarwood tree. It is one of the most expensive raw materials in the world, prized for its deep, woody, and animalic complexity. In our blends, we use only ethically sourced Oud that provides a long-lasting, majestic foundation for those who seek a scent with true authority.

Saffron: The Red Gold

Saffron adds a leathery, slightly sweet, and earthy nuance that is unmistakable. Harvested by hand from the Crocus sativus flower, it brings a golden warmth to the fragrance, reminiscent of the glowing desert sun at dusk. It is the thread that weaves our oriental notes together.

The Harmony of Tradition

Beyond individual ingredients, Arab Glow is about the harmony of tradition. We balance the intensity of spices with the softness of Turkish Rose and the sweetness of Honey. The result is a scent that is both powerful and refined, ancient and contemporary.

Experience the warmth of the Orient with Hyscent, and let your presence be felt long after you leave the room.
